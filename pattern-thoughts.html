The silence between words is where meaning lives

We fill our days with noise—notifications, opinions, the endless scroll. But meaning doesn't arrive announced. It slips in during the pause. The breath between sentences. The moment before you respond. Today, notice where you rush to fill the quiet. What might be waiting there?
